Demand Forecasting

Our Consulting Solution on Demand Planning focuses on providing better demand visibility to the supply chain and enable them to be customer-driven and demand driven.

Our Consulting solution will help you as a client -

  • Establish the ROI in developing the demand planning capability within the organization
  • Design the Demand Model and develop the demand consensus process
  • To identify the best in class solution that will match your business needs
  • Implement the technology and tune the solution including optimizing the Statistical Algorithms
  • Develop the monthly process that involves Pre-consensus, demand modeling, and consensus and the final forecast release to the Supply Chain

    • The demand planning process should be designed to deliver an accurate forecast - right product, right time and right location!
