Supply Chain Assesment

Assess end to end supply chain planning process:

  • Review current demand and supply planning process and system requirements
  • Efficient frontier analysis from analyzing inventory policies within existing supply chain structure and processes
  • Evaluate tradeoffs against key fulfillment process drivers: segmentation , replenishment policies, mix optimization etc

 

Evaluate strategic scenarios and potential for breakthrough improvements:
  • Soft changes: How service and inventory turns cab be improved by tuning the replenishment & stocking policies
  • Hard changes: Evaluate impact of network design changes
The Diagnostic examines the "as-is" process and compares it to the benchmarks and your industry accepted best practices to highlight areas of opportunity. We conduct a rapid diagnostic audit by studying key Performance Measures, build them where necessary, and by interviewing key employees and stakeholders.
